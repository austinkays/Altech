💬 Ask About Your Policy

AI answers with section citations & confidence scores

Upload a policy document above, then ask any question below.

ℹ️ How It Works

Desktop (Tauri): Policies are chunked, embedded, and stored in a local ChromaDB vector database on your machine. Questions use semantic search to find relevant sections, then an LLM generates answers with page/section citations.

Browser (Stateless): Policy text is extracted client-side and sent per-question to the AI. No data is persisted beyond your browser session. Suitable for quick lookups.

⚠️ Disclaimer: AI answers are informational only. Always verify coverage details with the carrier or a licensed professional. Confidence scores reflect the AI's certainty, not legal guarantees.

🚀 Auto-Fill EZLynx

Opens Chromium, waits for login, then fills the current page with matching data.

🔑 Quick Login Stored locally only
ℹ️ How It Works ▼

Option A — Chrome Extension (Recommended):
1. Click 📤 Copy for Extension to copy client data.
2. Open EZLynx in your browser.
3. Click the Altech extension → 📋 Paste from Altech.
4. Navigate to each form page → click ⚡ Fill This Page.

Option B — Python Filler (Desktop):
1. Click 🔄 Refresh — log in, navigate pages, dropdowns auto-scrape.
2. Enter client data or click 📥 Intake.
3. Click Auto-Fill → Chromium opens → log in → Fill Now on each page.

Both methods fuzzy-match dropdowns: WA → Washington, HO3 → HO3 - Dwelling, Commute → To/From Work.

💡 Chrome Extension: Load from chrome://extensions → Enable Developer Mode → Load Unpacked → select chrome-extension/.
