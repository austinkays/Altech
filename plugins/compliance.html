How CGL Tracking Works

Overview

This dashboard tracks commercial policy expirations (CGL, Bond, BOP, etc.) from HawkSoft. It flags policies approaching expiration so you can take action — notify clients, update state websites, and mark records as renewed.

Status Badges

X daysDays until expiration (≤90 days shown)
ExpiredPolicy is past its expiration date
⚠️ RenewedHawkSoft shows a new expiration 30+ days out — likely renewed. Requires external update.
✅ State UpdatedYou confirmed the state website was updated (CGL). Still need to click Updated/Dismiss to hide.
✅ HawkSoft UpdatedYou confirmed HawkSoft was updated (Bond). Still need to click Updated/Dismiss to hide.

🔄 Renewal Cycle

This repeats every year for each policy:

1Policy approaches expiration — appears in the dashboard with a countdown badge
2Carrier renews — HawkSoft data updates with a new expiration date
3Dashboard flags it — the ⚠️ Renewed badge appears
4You update externally — update the state website (CGLs) or HawkSoft (bonds), then mark it done
5Updated/Dismiss — hide the policy until next year's renewal
⚠️ Every renewal is flagged — no expiration change is ever silently skipped.

⚖️ Two-Step Workflow

Step 1: Click �️ State Updated (CGLs) or 🦅 HawkSoft Updated (bonds) to mark the external update. This adds a ✅ badge but does NOT hide the policy.
Step 2: Click Updated or Dismiss to hide it until next year.
UpdatedHides the policy. Saves expiration as the baseline for next renewal detection.
DismissHides the policy. It resurfaces if HawkSoft detects a renewal (>30 day expiration change).

Tip: A soft warning appears if you Updated without marking State/HawkSoft Updated first — you can override it.
