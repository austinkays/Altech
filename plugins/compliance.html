📋 How CGL Tracking Works

Overview

This dashboard tracks commercial policy expirations (CGL, Bond, BOP, etc.) pulled from your HawkSoft account. It flags policies approaching expiration so you can notify insureds and update your state website records when policies renew.

Status Badges

Expires tomorrowPolicy expires within 1 day
X daysDays until expiration (≤90 days shown)
ExpiredPolicy is past its expiration date
⚠️ RenewedExpiration changed by >30 days — likely renewed. Requires action: update your state website, then click "State Updated"
✅ State UpdatedYou confirmed the state website was updated. Policy is auto-dismissed and will resurface at next renewal.

🔄 Renewal Detection Cycle

This is the core workflow that repeats every year for each policy:

  1. Policy approaches expiration — appears in the dashboard with a countdown badge
  2. You dismiss or verify it — policy is hidden from the active list
  3. Carrier renews the policy — HawkSoft data updates with a new expiration date
  4. Dashboard detects the renewal — expiration changed >30 days from the stored baseline. The ⚠️ Renewed badge appears automatically
  5. You update the state website — then click 🏛️ State Updated
  6. Policy auto-dismisses — new expiration is saved as the baseline for next year
  7. Cycle repeats — when the new term renews, it resurfaces again

⚠️ Important: No policy ever gets its expiration updated silently. Every renewal is flagged with the "⚠️ Renewed" badge so you never miss one.

📝 Notes & Quick Actions

Click the 📝 button on any policy to expand the note panel. Quick-add buttons:

📞 Notified InsuredLog that you called the insured about the expiration
📧 Emailed InsuredLog that you sent an email
📱 Left VoicemailLog a voicemail attempt
✅ Renewal ConfirmedThe insured confirmed their policy renewed
🔄 Renewed (New Policy #)Enter the new policy number — links old and new
🏛️ State UpdatedMark that you updated the state website — auto-dismisses the policy
🛏️ SleepTemporarily hide the policy until tomorrow

The emoji icons (📞📧🔄 etc.) appear in each row so you can see at a glance what actions were taken without opening the notes.

⚖️ Updated Toggle vs Dismiss vs State Updated

These three actions look similar but behave differently:

Updated (toggle)The checkbox in the UPDATED column. Marks the policy as verified/done and hides it from the active list. Does NOT save a renewal baseline — use this for policies you've reviewed but that haven't renewed (e.g., expired and not renewing).
Dismiss (button)Hides the policy from the active list. It resurfaces automatically if HawkSoft detects a renewal (expiration changes >30 days). Use this when you've reviewed a policy and want it out of the way — it will come back when it matters again.
🏛️ State UpdatedThe button inside the notes panel. Confirms you updated your state website with the renewal info. Auto-dismisses AND saves the current expiration as the baseline for next year's renewal detection. This is the recommended action after a policy renews.

Rule of thumb: After a renewal → use 🏛️ State Updated. For everything else → use Dismiss or the Updated toggle.

Other Row Actions

🛏️ SnoozeHide until tomorrow. Use "Show Hidden" to see snoozed policies.
Show HiddenToggles visibility of all dismissed, verified, and snoozed policies (shown dimmed with strikethrough)

Deduplication

Same policy number: If HawkSoft returns multiple terms for the same policy number, only the newest expiration is kept. The older term is merged automatically.

Cross-number renewals: If the same client has an expired policy AND an active policy of the same type, the expired one is auto-dismissed as "superseded" and linked to the active policy.

🖨 Print & 💾 Backup

Print: Click 🖨 Print to enter selection mode. Check the policies you want, then click "Generate PDF" to create a printable report.

Backup: Click 💾 Backup to download all your annotations (verified, dismissed, notes) as a JSON file. Use "📂 Restore Backup" at the bottom of the page to import it back — data is merged, never overwritten.
